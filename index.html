Firmer

30 minutes • From £35 a session

Radio frequency is one of several 3D liposuction procedures. It involves the use of radio waves to tighten the skin around the treated area; if, for example, you would like your abdomen to look more toned, the radio frequency treatment will help.

i-Lipo

FAT REDUCTION

20 minutes • From £50

The i-Lipo emits low levels of laser energy, which creates a chemical signal in the fat cells, breaking down the stored triglycerides into free fatty acids and glycerol and releasing them though channels in the cell membranes. The fatty acids and glycerol are then transported around the body to the tissues that will use them during metabolism to create energy. This process of fatty acid release is a natural response of the body when the body needs to used stored energy reserves, thus i-Lipo is not creating any unnatural reaction in the body nor does it affect or damage any surrounding structures such as skin, blood vessels and peripheral nerves.

A period of exercise post treatment will ensure the complete metabolism and thus elimination from the body of the freed fatty acids.

Clinical studies show that i-lipo has comparable results that are achieved through liposuction surgery. There is a 30% reduction in the fat layer depth just after one treatment, and more can be achieved through more sessions. As the fat cell contents are released and excreted through the lymphatic system, immediate results can be seen after the treatment. It is recommended to do light exercise post treatment to accelerate the removal process of the released fat.

Fat Freeze

Fat Reduction

40 minutes • From £59

Fat Freeze is a popular fat reduction treatment for both men and women as it is capable of targeting specific areas of unwanted fat. It does not require surgery, making it an ideal alternative to liposuction for removing stubborn fat which proves difficult to lose by weight loss diets and exercise alone.

This no invasive procedure uses a process called cryolipolysis to sculpt the body. The applicator gently draws the unwanted fat into a vacuum and exposes the fat cells to very low temperatures, damaging them. These dead fat cells are then naturally processed by the body and removed.
